I am a PhD student at [ShanghaiTech University](https://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn/), advised by [Xin Liu](https://liuxincell.github.io/). My research lies at the intersection of reinforcement learning and bandits, with a particular emphasis on both theoretical foundations and applications to large language models and resource allocation.

In May 2025, I was honored to be selected for the Tencent Rhino-Bird Elite Talent Program, where I worked on the intersection of reinforcement learning and recommender systems under the guidance of [Junwei Pan](https://junwei-pan.github.io/).

I am currently interested in reinforcement learning for agentic LLMs and multimodal large models, as well as embodied intelligence, and I am open to related opportunities.
