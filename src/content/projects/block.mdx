BLOCK is an open-source bi-stage MLLM pipeline for translating character concepts into Minecraft-ready skins. The system first synthesizes structured preview images and then converts them into valid skin atlases with a deterministic decoder.

I'm iterating and working on generating Minecraft skins end-to-end. If you are also interested, feel free to contact me.
